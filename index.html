Maxwell Aladago

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at Dartmouth College working with Dr. Lorenzo Torresani and Dr.Soroush Vosoughi on the intersection of computer vision and language in weakly/self-supervised learning. I earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from Ashesi University in 2018. My undergraduate thesis centered on the use of deep learning methods to improve malaria diagnosis.

Recently, I interned at Google Research where I worked on novel mult-modal fusion methods for vision and language tasks.

Compound Tokens: Channel Fusion for Vision-Language Representation Learning
Maxwell Aladago, AJ Piergiovanni

arXiv

Constructs strong representations for visual question answering tasks by fusing vision and language tokens along the channel dimension using cross-attention.
